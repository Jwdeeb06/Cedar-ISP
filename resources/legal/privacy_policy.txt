1. INTRODUCTION
---------------
This Privacy Policy explains how CedarISP collects, stores,
uses, and protects information when the CedarISP desktop
application is installed, accessed, or used.

CedarISP is a desktop software application designed for
Internet Service Provider business management. It includes
features for subscriber management, invoices, payments, wallet
balances, cash drawer records, POS operations, employees,
permissions, activity logs, local backups, network mapping,
prepared WhatsApp messages, and license validation.

CedarISP is intended for business and professional use only.

By installing or using CedarISP, you agree to this Privacy
Policy.


2. WHO CONTROLS THE DATA
------------------------
The business or person using CedarISP is responsible for the
business data entered into the application. This includes
subscriber data, customer data, employee data, invoices,
payments, POS records, drawer records, network map
information, exports, backups, and any other local business
records.

Jawad Deeb does not control, monitor, or access the local
database stored on the client's computer, except where the
client voluntarily provides access during support.

Jawad Deeb only processes limited license validation and
support-related information as described in this Privacy
Policy.


3. LOCAL DATA STORED BY CEDARISSP
----------------------------------
CedarISP stores most application data locally on the client's
computer using a local SQLite database. This local data may
include, but is not limited to:

Subscriber Data:
  - Subscriber ID, username, full name
  - PPPoE / connection password
  - Mobile number, address, region, building, notes
  - Service plan, reseller, collector, switch name
  - MAC address, nationality, coordinates
  - Price, balance, quotas, expiry date
  - Blocked/account status, role, archive status
  - Company/provider link, timestamps

POS Customer Data:
  - Customer name, phone number, email
  - Purchase history, unpaid balance
  - POS invoices and payment records

Employee Data:
  - Username, password hash (never plain text), role
  - Permissions and activity records

Financial and Business Data:
  - Invoices, payments, wallet transactions
  - Cash drawer transactions, POS sales and refunds
  - POS stock, service plans, companies, reports
  - Activity logs

Map and Network Data:
  - Subscriber coordinates, stations, fiber boxes
  - Network map markers, default map location settings


4. LICENSE VALIDATION DATA
--------------------------
CedarISP validates the license online on each login.

During license validation, CedarISP may send and store:
  - Login or validation time
  - License name, client name, or license identifier
  - Public IP address
  - Validation result (success or failure)
  - Basic request or security metadata

This data is used for license validation, license
enforcement, fraud prevention, abuse detection, security
monitoring, support, and preventing unauthorized use.

License validation is required. If the license cannot be
validated, access to the application may be blocked.


5. PUBLIC IP ADDRESS
--------------------
CedarISP collects the public IP address during license
validation to verify license activity, detect abuse, protect
the license system, and support clients. The public IP address
is not used to access the client's local database.


6. LICENSE LOG RETENTION
------------------------
License validation logs may be kept for as long as reasonably
necessary for license enforcement, fraud prevention, abuse
detection, security, troubleshooting, support, legal
protection, and business records. Logs may be deleted or
minimized when no longer needed.


7. NO ACCESS TO LOCAL CLIENT DATABASE
--------------------------------------
Jawad Deeb does not automatically receive, upload, copy, or
access the local CedarISP database. The client's subscriber
data, invoices, payments, POS records, employee records, and
local business data remain on the client's device unless the
client chooses to export, share, transfer, back up, or provide
them for support.


8. SUPPORT ACCESS
-----------------
Standard support hours:
  Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM Lebanon time
  (excluding holidays and unavailable periods)

Remote support is provided only when the client voluntarily
grants access. Sensitive or urgent support outside these hours
may require an additional fee. The client is responsible for
ensuring they are authorized to share any information provided
during support.


9. LOCAL BACKUPS
----------------
CedarISP may create automatic local backups on startup,
keeping approximately the last seven days. Backups are stored
locally on the client's computer, typically at:

  C:\Users\[User]\AppData\Roaming\isp-management-system\backups

Backup files may contain sensitive business, subscriber,
customer, employee, payment, POS, and financial information.
The client is responsible for protecting, copying, deleting,
securing, and restoring local backup files.


10. DATA EXPORTS
----------------
CedarISP may allow import and export of data including Excel
files. Exported files may contain sensitive information. Once
exported, the client is fully responsible for that data.


11. WHATSAPP MESSAGES
---------------------
CedarISP may prepare WhatsApp messages and redirect the client
to WhatsApp or a related service. CedarISP does not send bulk
spam and does not store sent message history unless separately
configured. The client is responsible for all message content,
recipient consent, and compliance with WhatsApp terms and
applicable laws.


12. PAYMENT AND FINANCIAL DATA
-------------------------------
CedarISP does not process online card payments. The app
records cash and manual payments entered by the client or
employees. CedarISP does not intentionally store credit card
numbers, bank card details, or online banking credentials.


13. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
-------------------------
CedarISP may use or interact with:
  - A private license validation system (developer-controlled)
  - Hosting/infrastructure providers for license validation
  - GitHub or website hosting for software download
  - Map services such as OpenStreetMap
  - WhatsApp or related services (message redirect only)
  - Remote support tools (when voluntarily used)

Third-party services are governed by their own privacy
policies. Jawad Deeb is not responsible for third-party
availability, policies, security, or actions.


14. SECURITY
------------
CedarISP uses reasonable technical measures including local
database storage and password hashing. No software can be
guaranteed completely secure. The client is responsible for
protecting their device, using strong passwords, restricting
employee permissions, protecting backup and exported files,
and using antivirus and device security tools.


15. DATA DELETION
-----------------
Most CedarISP data is stored locally. The client is
responsible for deleting or managing local data. Uninstalling
CedarISP may not automatically delete all databases, backups,
exports, or configuration files. The client should manually
verify and delete remaining files if desired.


16. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES
----------------------------
The client is responsible for:
  - Obtaining legally required consent from subscribers,

  - Entering accurate data
  - Securing devices and backups
  - Managing employee access and permissions
  - Complying with privacy, telecom, employment, accounting,
